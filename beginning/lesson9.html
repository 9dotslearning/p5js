In this lesson, you'll use images from the Internet to create animated scenes like the example below, where a fish swims under a sunny sky. After you learn the basic skills, you can make any scene you wish.

To create scenes, you'll need to work in the p5.js editor. Our little Tinker windows don't work with images, unfortunately.

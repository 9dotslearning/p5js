Magic's First p5.js

Welcome. These pages share lessons in p5.js, a computer programming language that draws animated art, plays music, and makes games on the web. Here are some examples of what you'll create: [[TBD embed]]

Written especially for middle school students, the lessons focus on learning by tinkering. They use concrete examples and take small steps. They offer plenty of time for experimentation before focusing on formal ideas like syntax (the proper order of commands and symbols), structure, and the notional machine that runs the code. Skills and concepts spiral across lessons, re-examined repeatedly. Most importantly, the examples and projects each contain a little magic, I hope, because learning seems best when magic's first.

STUDENTS NEED TO CREATE ACCOUNTS AND MEET THE P5.JS ONLINE EDITOR, STEP BY STEP. THEY ALSO NEED A FRAMEWORK TO PUT P5.JS INTO PERSPECTIVE.

A computer programming language is a set of words and symbols that you can use to control what your computer does. There are hundreds languages, and each one has unique strengths. p5.js is especially good at making web-based creative projects, but it has many other capabilities as well.

p5.js is closely related to several other languages. It's a specialized version (called a library) of JavaScript, which makes web pages respond to clicks, mouse movements, time, date, location, and many other events. In addition, p5.js is closely related to a language called Processing, which, like p5.js, makes creative projects. But Processing projects are more difficult to create and change on the web, so we'll focus on p5.js instead.

Answers

createCanvas(640, 480) draws a window (the canvas) that is 640 pixels wide and 480 pixels high. A pixel is the smallest dot on the screen that can show color and measures about 1/100 inch. So our canvas is about 6.4 inches wide x 4.8 inches tall.

background(110) paints the canvas gray color 110. Shades of gray range from 0 (black) to 255 (white), with grays getting lighter as numbers increase.

ellipse(400, 150, 50) draws a circle with horizontal position at 400 pixels across and 150 pixels down the screen. The circle is 50 pixels wide.
